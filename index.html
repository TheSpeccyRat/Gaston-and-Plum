A note from us

Hello — we're so glad you found us

We started this because we wanted to remember it all — the perfect pitches, the wrong turns down single-track lanes, the sunsets over the sea wall with a glass of something cold. If you're curious about life on the road, whether you're dreaming of it or already living it, pull up a camping chair. You're very welcome here.

— with love, from Gaston & Plum

Our trusty home on wheels. A VW Transporter with a personality bigger than his wheelbase. Gaston goes wherever we point him — and has never once complained about an early start or a muddy pitch.
